About the Artist

Jesse White is a narrative expressionist painter, photographer, poet, altered book artist, and teacher from Philadelphia, PA. She is the Founder and Director of Pigeon Arts.

Jesse creates with the intention of sharing an emotion with the viewer. She finds the process of making art spiritually rich.

Her work has been exhibited throughout the Greater Philadelphia area, North Carolina, London, England and Medellín, Colombia.

Jesse's art and teaching is focused on social change. A Leeway Foundation Art & Social Change Award recipient, Jesse has facilitated creative programming to participants of all ages. To learn more about Pigeon Arts' educational program offerings, please visit: pigeon-arts.org.
